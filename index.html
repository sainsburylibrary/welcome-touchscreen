Opening Hours

If you are an SBS student with an SBS access card, you can enter the library whenever the Park End Street building is open! Currently this is 8am-10.30pm.

Library staff are available to help during term time M-F 9am-7pm. (Vacation M-F 9am-5pm).

Wi-Fi and IT

Use Eduroam if you are a member of the University. The Cloud Wi-Fi network is available for anyone without an Oxford Single Sign On.

PCs and iMacs are available for SBS students on both floors. Use your SBS network login.
